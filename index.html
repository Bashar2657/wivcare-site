Why Wi-VCare

Protection that respects the person it protects.

Most monitoring asks seniors to wear something, charge something, or be watched. Wi-VCare asks for nothing — it simply senses the home around them.

Always on, never worn

The system senses the environment automatically. There is nothing to remember, wear, or recharge.

It senses life

Beyond motion, Wi-VCare reads breathing — so it can tell a dangerous fall from someone resting on the sofa.

Private by design

No images are ever recorded. Only signal patterns are analysed — making it GDPR-compliant from the ground up.

How it works

One device. The whole home.

WiFi passes through walls — so a single unit covers the rooms where falls happen most, including the bathroom and bedroom.

Sense

Wi-VCare reads the tiny disturbances a body makes in everyday WiFi signals — presence, movement, even respiration.

Understand

An AI model distinguishes a real fall from normal daily activity, reducing false alarms while catching true emergencies.

Alert

If someone falls and stays still, family or care services are notified automatically — even if the person is unconscious.

Growing older at home should never mean trading away dignity for safety.
